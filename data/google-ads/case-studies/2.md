A global refurbished electronics marketplace operating across multiple European markets, offering customers the ability to buy, sell, and trade devices online and in-store.

### The Client's Concern

The client was dealing with overlapping transaction data, inaccurate offline conversion tracking, and campaign overlap across multiple initiatives, making performance difficult to interpret


### How We Helped

$$$ Custom Tracking Implementation

Introduced a tracking setup that improved ROAS reporting clarity and made scaling decisions more reliable



$$$ Offline Conversion Tracking

Built a strategy to better measure the true impact of store visit campaigns

The result: Even though impressions dropped, our Click-Through Rate (CTR) skyrocketed by 131%. This showed the new keywords were a perfect match for what customers were searching for. We generated 33% more clicks while spending slightly less per click. We ensured spending was focused entirely on high-value users, leading to a 57% increase in purchases.

$$$ Campaign Restructuring

Restructured campaigns to reduce overlap, improve efficiency, and create cleaner campaign-level insights

### The Final Result

The new setup improved tracking accuracy, reduced campaign overlap, and gave clearer visibility across online and offline performance. Campaign management also became more streamlined, making optimisation and scaling noticeably easier..
