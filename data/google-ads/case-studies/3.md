A leading healthcare provider looking to increase patient inquiries and improve online conversions

### The Client's Concern

The hospital was attracting online traffic, but a large portion of visitors were leaving without taking action. Existing campaigns were generating clicks, yet inquiries were not growing at the same pace. The client needed a more efficient way to connect with people actively searching for healthcare services.


### How We Helped

$$$ Refined the overall digital approach to improve conversion quality. 
$$$ Shifted focus toward attracting more relevant audiences.
$$$ Took steps to improve and simplify the user’s online journey.
$$$ Prioritised better-performing traffic.


### The Final Result

The hospital saw a significant increase in qualified patient inquiries while improving overall cost efficiency. The updated strategy also created a smoother experience.
